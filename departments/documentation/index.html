Documentation

The documentation department at Penelope is responsible for creating and managing technical and non-technical documents related to software development projects. Their main goal is to ensure that all documents are accurate, up-to-date, and easily accessible to all members.

They work closely with all departments to create technical documentation such as the functional specification and user manuals. they also create non-technical documentation such as project plans, meeting minutes, and other documents. The documentation department plays a critical role in ensuring that projects are well-documented and that members have access to the information they need to make informed decisions. Effective document management helps to improve collaboration, reduce errors, and increase efficiency.
