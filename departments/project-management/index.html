Project Management

Project Management

The project management department at Penelope LTD oversees software development projects for clients. they work closely with the all the departments, plan and manage resources, and communicate progress and changes to all members of our team. their responsibilities include identifying project requirements, assessing risks, and creating a roadmap for development. They aim to deliver a product that meets client needs and expectations.

Effective resource management ensures projects are completed on time and within budget. Communication with clients and team members helps to ensure everyone is informed and projects stay on track. The project management department plays a critical role in ensuring successful project completion.

Project workflow

To simplify the product development process, Penelope splits the project lifecycle into 5 distinct phases that provide the project with more predictability.

The first phase consists of the Initiation Phase where the project is defined and a product vision is created.

The second phase (Planning Phase) is the phase where all the goals and details are defined in order to complete what has been set on the first phase.

The Execution Phase is characterised by the execution of the product, when all that has been defined comes to life.

During the Control & Monitoring Phase, which happens alongside the previous phase, the Project manager is responsible for monitoring progress and making adjustments if necessary, ensuring that the product is aligned with what was defined in the project plan.

At last, the Closure Phase dictates the end of the project and during this phase Penelope ensures that the product meets all the requirements defined in the first phase.
